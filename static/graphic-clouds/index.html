What does it do?

Description how this art is generated.

Central concept

It is any word of your choosing. The program is going to seach the web for this concept and collect all the other words and images closely associated with it. No search information is saved, each new request may potentially result in completely different data.

Customizing

Words and images found by the program are going to be mapped around or inside the mask created by the central concept. You can choose the final size, color scheme, additional effects and word/image ratio to customize the resulting visual outlook.

Momentary art

Download the final image as you see it. Next day it won't be avalable anymore. Thus each image is a unique and fleeting depiction of one moment in time expressed via text. A momentary slice of our collective subconsciousness.
