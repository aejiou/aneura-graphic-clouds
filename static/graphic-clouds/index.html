What does it do?

Description how these images are generated.

Central concept

It is any word of your choice. The program is going to seach the web for it and collect all the other words and images closely associated with it. No search information is saved, each new request may potentially result in completely different data.

Customizing

Words found by the program are going to be mapped around or inside the mask created by the central concept. You can provide additional keywords to clarify the meaning. Colors will be inferred from the images found with the same search. Just choose the font style.

Momentary art

Download the final image as you see it. Next day it won't be avalable anymore. Thus each image is a unique and fleeting depiction of one moment in time expressed via text. A momentary and random slice of our collective subconsciousness the way Google knows it.
